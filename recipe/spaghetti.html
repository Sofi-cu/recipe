Spaghetti Carbonara

Description:

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, savory, and utterly delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 spring onions, finely chopped
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.
  5. While the chicken is resting, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  6. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  7. In the same skillet used for the chicken, melt the butter over medium heat.
  8. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  9. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally. Allow it to simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
  10. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  11. Add the sliced chicken to the Alfredo sauce and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until everything is heated through.
  12. Serve the Chicken Alfredo over the cooked fettuccine pasta, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
